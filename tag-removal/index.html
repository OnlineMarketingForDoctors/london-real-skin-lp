Is it right for you

Signs you are a great candidate for ACP

  • You have skin tags, milia, cherry angiomas or age spots
  • A lesion catches on clothing, jewellery or when shaving
  • The blemish is benign but bothers you day to day
  • You want a quick treatment with minimal downtime
  • You would prefer a dermatology-led, regulated setting
  • You are in good general health with settled skin
Some lesions need a dermatologist to give the all-clear before treatment. Working alongside Dr Wade and the consultant dermatologists at The London Skin and Hair Clinic means any referral is simple, and it is all discussed at your complimentary skin analysis.
Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Most patients describe a quick, warm pinprick sensation rather than pain. Treatments are short, and where an area is more sensitive we can apply a topical numbing cream first to keep you comfortable throughout.

Many skin tags and small lesions are removed in a single session. Larger, more numerous or more stubborn lesions can occasionally need a follow-up. Your practitioner will give you a realistic plan at your consultation.

ACP treats the lesion precisely while protecting the surrounding skin, so scarring is uncommon and downtime is minimal. You may see a small scab or slight redness that settles over the following days. We give you full aftercare to support healing.

Once a lesion is properly treated it does not usually return. However, if you are prone to skin tags or cherry angiomas, new ones can appear elsewhere over time. These can be treated again just as easily.

ACP is suitable for many skin types, and your practitioner will always assess your skin individually. In some cases a patch test or a dermatologist review is recommended first, which we can arrange in-house before any treatment.

Anything that has changed in size, shape or colour, or that your practitioner wants a specialist opinion on, is reviewed by one of our dermatologists before treatment. It is a simple referral within the clinic, and it is there entirely for your safety and peace of mind.
